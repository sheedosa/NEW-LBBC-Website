Council Membership for Libya-registered

1. Complete the online application form, tick the box to say you have read our Privacy Policy, and click SUBMIT.

2. You will be taken to a secure payments page where you can make a payment online, using your credit or debit card.

3. Alternatively you can choose the PAY BY INVOICE option, and we will send you an invoice that you can pay by whichever method you prefer.

Please note that your membership will become active only after payment has been received.
